Enjoy the moment of my life

Why do I cherish MOMENT & PHOTO?

When I was little, my dearest Grandpa past away. Until recent years, I just found out we don't even have a photo together and my memory of him started to blur. That became one of the great regrets of my life and I was worried what if I lost the memory of our moments together completely.

Brain is not trustworthy, but CAMERA IS.
I often think back of a moment in my life, then I found camera, when the shutter button's pressed, that moment can be preserved forever. Lens are my eyes, they can frame the view I see; Photos are my mouth, they can share the voice of mine to anyone who's willing to communicate with me.
