我想，慢慢长大

全世界都在催促我长大，只有迪士尼还在守护我内心的美好童话
Teenage is the most confusing age I've ever met, sometimes I was been treated like an adult, sometimes like a child. For most of times, I lost the define of myself.
But I do know, when the whole world is pushing me to grow up, I may take a rest at Disneyland and enjoy it just like a naive child.
