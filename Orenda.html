ORENDA


Short vacation with my family

Due to the COVID-19, Government made a regulation that students are not allowed to leave their city 14 days before the school term starts. Therefore, my parents decided to take us to a nearby island - CHONGMING for a two days vacation just before the school starts. ORENDA, is the name of the vacation campus that we visited, as it's almost the work day, the only visitors there were us and the nature.
I enjoyed the precious time without pressures from studies, univerisities, etc...
